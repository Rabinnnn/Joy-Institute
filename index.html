Course/Learning levels, Durations and Fees

Fees for hairdressing courses and beauty therapy are as follows:

  • Certificate in Nail Technology - Ksh.30,000 for 2 months.
  • Diploma in Holistic Therapy - Ksh.50,000 for 4 months.
  • Diploma in Barbering - Ksh.35,000 for 3 months.
  • Diploma in Hairdressing - Ksh.75,500 for 6 months.

Obtaining a degree from diploma and Credits transfer

The fees for upgrade to bachelors degree depends on the University and is further based on Credit Transfer under The council for Accreditation of Experiential Learning (ACAEL) WWW.cael-africa.org

It must be mentioned that you can continue with these programmes to masters or doctoral degree level. For upgrade to any degree level, please write an email to ACAEL (info@cael-africa.org)
